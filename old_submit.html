submissions



We are accepting films, TV pilots, and screenplays! Submissions are processed through Withoutabox. Withoutabox account holders get a $5 discount on all submission fees. Email programming@ivyfilmfestival.com with any questions about submissions or submission fees, and email director@ivyfilmfestival.com with any general questions about the festival itself.

Submissions are accepted online through Withoutabox. Filmmakers and screenwriters must create their own basic Withoutabox accounts to submit. This process is free and easy – simply follow the “Submit” link on the bottom of this page. Submitters can also elect for “premium” Withoutabox accounts and receive a $5 discount on their entry fees. Fees are identical for all domestic film, international film, and screenplay competitions.

To be eligible for consideration,your submission must have been created while you were enrolled at a college/university.
