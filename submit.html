Email programming@ivyfilmfestival.org with any questions about submissions or submission fees, screenplay@ivyfilmfestival.org with any questions related to screenplay submissions and email director@ivyfilmfestival.org with any general questions about the festival itself.

If you would like to host a satellite festival and waive your school's submission fees, email outreach@ivyfilmfestival.org

Ivy Film Festival (IFF) is the largest student-run film festival in the world and offers students from domestic and foreign universities a venue to exhibit their work.
