donate

Please consider donating to the Ivy Film Festival. We use donations to help fund our educational programming, activities, and workshops to participating student artists, industry guests, and attending audience members during both satellite and festival week

how to donate

We accept donations though the official Brown Giving website. To donate to the Ivy Film Festival, please follow the steps below:

  1. Visit the Brown Giving website

  2. Click "Give Now" in the top right corner

  3. Select "Other". If "Other" is not visible, search for "Ivy Film Festival".

  4. Once you have selected "Other", in the section where it asks to specify your gift, please type "Ivy Film Festival, SAC638795"

Ivy Film Festival (IFF) is among the largest student-run film festival in the world and offers students from domestic and foreign universities a venue to exhibit their work.
